My Favorite Dog Breeds!

Siberian Husky

 A Siberian Husky, according to American Kennel Club, Siberian Huskies have great endurance, especially because of their thick coat. They are also very friendly, making them perfect for families.

Golden Retriever

 These dogs are very easy to handle, altough they can be very playful and sometimes naughty. Dogbible also stated that "It is considered to be of a peaceful and balanced nature."

Alaskan Malamute

 According to American Kennel Club, they are very strong and a heavy-duty worker. They are known for working very hard usually as a sled dog. Despite the intimidating characteristics, they can also be playful and gentle dogs, especially with kids.

Bull Terrier

 Bull Terriers are devoted yet playful and endearing dogs, according to American Kenner Club. They have unique heads that are different from other breeds. The ideal Bull Terrier would be a muscular and well-trained dog.
